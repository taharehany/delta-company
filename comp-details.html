Aljazea Ductile Fitting Factory

  • Feb 14

In line with urban and cultural development in the Kingdom and the region as well as the vision Aljazea industrial to diversify its Industrial portfolio, we have established Aljazea construction chemical plant (JCC) in 2007 for the produc- tion and manufacture of construction chemicals using the best equipment and industrial production at the highest level of technology and the of international standards, so as to meet the needs of the local and international markets. The availability of raw materials economically in the Kingdom has helped to estab- lish this kind of industries. Though, due to the market urgent needs for products contend with imported products we have established Aljazea construction chemical plant (JCC), on the basis of providing the required levels of quality with competitive price. The factory has achieved rapid growth to become one of the most important producers of construction chemicals and a major produc- er of concrete additives, waterproofing systems, epoxy paints and cement, as well as adhesives and glue, beside the concrete injection materials.

The company’s products that have gained excellent reputation as a result of the high quality and continuous development to production inputs are: • Special- ized hospitals, medical centers, water tanks paints. • Chemical additives to improve the concrete. • Chemical additives to improve the final surface treat- ment of concrete. • Chemical additives for the treatment of concrete cracks. • Chemical additives for flooring factories to give it strength and high hardness. • Chemical products specialized in iron paint that protects it and treatment it from the rust. • Chemical products for the treatment and prevention of leakage in permeability reservoirs and swimming pools. Special material for the treat- ment of salts and waterproofing.

Aljazea construction chemical plant (1CC) is committed to institutional and professional vision, which helped us to achieve the highest levels of customer satisfaction by offering products and services of with high level of efficiency and apply best manufacturing practices with the highest international stan- dards , as a result the factory has been qualified the for Total Quality Certificate (ISO 9001: 2000 ).
